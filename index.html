I am Jonnatan Arias Garcia, Professor at the University of Quindío and researcher at the Technological University of Pereira, I actively participate in the research of the UTP Automation Group. I am pursuing my doctorate with an emphasis on artificial intelligence. I am a scholarship recipient from the Colombian Ministry of Education.

My academic career has been marked by a deep fascination with knowledge and science. I developed my master's degree in the area of physics, specifically in astronomy and astrophysics. Where the research I developed was around analyzing one of the most devastating and extremely strange explosions in the universe, called GRB's (gamma ray burst).

Currently, I am deeply involved in my research, which covers the areas of Deep Geometric Learning. My work involves generating novel methodologies and AI models that help with the description and volumetric comparison of 3D objects or shapes, focused on the medical area.

I'm always excited to talk about science, don't hesitate to contact me :)
